This website is the digital homeland of angel bby, a trickster harpist-bae character developed by contemporary harpist and composer Grace Scheele in collaboration with LJ Bullen for Grace's interdisciplinary multimedia solo show not yr angel bby harpist.

not yr angel bby harpist will premiere on June 4th, 2022 at 8pm in Kitchener, ON during Open Ears Festival of Music and Sound. Get your tickets here.

not yr angel bby harpist was developed by Grace Scheele during Inter Arts Matrix's 2021-2022 COVE/COVEX program with mentorship by Richard Burrows, Artistic Director of Open Ears Festival of Music and Sound.
